About Us

Delivering Lasting Impact Through Constituency Projects

Synergy Field Limited is a premier Abuja-based consulting and project management firm specializing in the implementation of constituency and zonal intervention projects for members of the Nigerian National Assembly.

Led by Engr. Chris Ugwu, we have successfully delivered hundreds of transformative projects — schools, hospitals, boreholes, rural roads, electrification, and skills training centers — across all 36 states and the FCT.

Our hallmark is transparency, technical excellence, and timely delivery — making us the preferred partner for lawmakers who want real results for their constituents.

Founded by Engr. Chris Ugwu with a vision to bridge the gap between legislative allocation and community development, Synergy Field has grown into a trusted name with zero abandoned projects in over 12 years.

To execute constituency projects with integrity, train legislators and staff for better governance, and deliver sustainable development to Nigerian communities.

To be Africa’s leading firm in legislative project implementation and governance capacity building by 2030.
