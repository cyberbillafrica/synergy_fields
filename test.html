Who We Are

A Trusted Partner in Constituency Project Delivery

Synergy Field Limited is a professional Nigerian consulting, project management and training firm headquartered in Abuja. We specialize in the planning, implementation, monitoring and evaluation of National Assembly constituency and zonal intervention projects across Nigeria.

Founded and led by Engr. Chris Ugwu, the company was established to bridge the gap between legislative intent and tangible community impact. Today, Synergy Field Limited is trusted by Senators, Members of the House of Representatives and legislative stakeholders nationwide.

Our operations span all six geo-political zones of Nigeria, supporting sustainable development through infrastructure delivery, capacity building and transparent project execution.

Our Experience

Proven Track Record Across Nigeria

With over a decade of hands-on experience, Synergy Field Limited has successfully delivered over 100 constituency and zonal intervention projects in urban and rural communities across Nigeria.

Our portfolio covers critical sectors including education, healthcare, water supply, rural infrastructure, renewable energy and electrification. We understand the political, technical and community-sensitive nature of constituency projects and manage each assignment with discretion, professionalism and accountability.
