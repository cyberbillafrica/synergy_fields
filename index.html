About Us

We Deliver Successful Constituency Projects Nationwide

Synergy Field Limited is a trusted Nigerian consulting and project management firm headquartered in Abuja. Under the leadership of Engr. Chris Ugwu (CEO), we specialize in the planning, implementation, monitoring and evaluation of constituency projects for Honourable Members and Senators of the Nigerian National Assembly.

With over a decade of experience, we have successfully executed numerous projects and training programs across all geo-political zones in Nigeria, delivering lasting impact to communities.

Founded and established by Engr. Chris Ugwu, Synergy Field Limited has grown to become a leading implementer of federal constituency and zonal intervention projects across Nigeria.

To provide professional, transparent and result-oriented project management and capacity-building services that empower legislators and transform communities.

To be the most trusted partner for constituency project execution and legislative training in Africa.
